If your lab is running physical experiments:
We are collecting videos of research activities to evaluate how well AI models can understand research intentions and goals in the lab!
Refer interested lab members to participate in:
1. Interview (30m) Orientation, instructions, and scheduling
2. Recording (2hr) Record two ~1-hour sessions in the lab
3. Reflection (30m) Interview about actions / intentions / goals in lab sessions
This research will produce a dataset of hands-on laboratory workflows for benchmarking AI models’ understanding of how actions connect to scientific goals and intentions.
